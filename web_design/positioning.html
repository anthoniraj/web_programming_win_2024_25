CSS Positioning and Display

Fixed: Positioned relative to the viewport, unaffected by scrolling.

Absolute: Positioned relative to the nearest positioned ancestor (non-static).

In CSS, top, bottom, left, and right are properties used to control the spacing, positioning, and sizing of elements relative to their surrounding elements.
